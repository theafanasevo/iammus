## The Conversation

There has been a lot of noise recently about how we build design systems. Are they too rigid? Do they kill creativity?

I found a particularly insightful thread on X that breaks down the balance between consistency and flexibility.

## Key Takeaways

The original discussion highlighted three main points:

1. **Accessibility First:** If it isn't accessible, it isn't a system, it's a style guide.
2. **Documentation is Code:** Treat your docs with the same versioning rigor as your components.
3. **Adoptability:** A system is only as good as its adoption rate among developers.

> "Design systems are not about controlling designers; they are about empowering them to move faster with confidence."
